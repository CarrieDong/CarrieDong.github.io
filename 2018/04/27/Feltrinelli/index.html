01.Background

This semester, I’m attending a ‘weird’ course-Management of Design Project. The reason I regard it as ‘weird’ is actually, the whole course is a service design in my opinion, not too much involved in ‘management’.

But anyway, it’s indeed interesting: your group select one brand which has an existing digital interface, then use the design-thinking methodology, to do researches, to dig deeeeeeeeep for customers’ problem. Your output should solve the problem no matter what exactly way you take. It could be a new application, a service improvement, or whatelse.

02.Start

The brand our group chose is ‘la Feltrinelli’-a very famous bookstore chain in italy. Yes, an italian brand for bookstore. I couldn’t understand how crazy we were at the beginning…such a very old and traditional industry.
